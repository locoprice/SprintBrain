Features

Six features. No filler.

Replaces TextBlaze, TextExpander, and Magical. Adds the two things they don't do: live formulas and reusable AI prompts.

⚡

Snippets

Trigger a snippet with a short tag like ::quoteEN. It expands into a full template. Multi-language support for EN, IT, ES with automatic fallback.

🧮

Formulas

Real math inside your snippets. Totals, discounts, fees, net prices — calculated when the snippet expands. CSP-safe parser, no eval.

🤖

AI Prompt Library

Reusable one-shot and few-shot prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, and any AI tool. Stored next to your snippets, expanded the same way.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Supabase-backed. Edits on one device show up on the others within a second.

📝

Notion Sync

Optional one-way or two-way sync with a Notion database. Edit snippets where your team already works.

📱

Mobile Access

Browse and copy your snippet library from your phone. No laptop required.
