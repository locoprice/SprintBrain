What's inside

Everything text expansion should be. Plus the two things it isn't.

A drop-in replacement for TextBlaze, TextExpander, and Magical — with live formulas and a reusable prompt library on top.

01 · Triggers

Type a short tag. Watch a full template appear.

::quoteEN expands into a 200-word multilingual quote. Works in Gmail, Notion, Slack, your CRM — anywhere you can type.

03 · Prompts

A library of reusable prompts.

One-shot and few-shot prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, and any model — stored next to your templates, expanded the same way.

04 · Cloud sync

Every device, under a second.

Backed by Supabase. Edit on your laptop, paste on your phone — propagated in < 1 s, encrypted at rest, no conflicts.

05 · Notion sync

Edit where your team already works.

Mirror a Notion database as your library — one-way or two-way. Reviewers comment in Notion; expansions update in the extension within the minute.

06 · Mobile

Your library, on any phone.

Browse, search, and copy from iOS and Android — no laptop required. Same library, same shortcuts, same formulas.
